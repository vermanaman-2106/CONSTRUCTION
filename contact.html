    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does a typical project take?

    Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A kitchen renovation typically takes 4-8 weeks, while a full home renovation can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

    Do you provide free consultations?

    Yes! We offer free initial consultations to discuss your project, understand your needs, and provide preliminary estimates. This helps us determine if we're a good fit for your project.

    Are you licensed and insured?

    Absolutely. We are fully licensed contractors with comprehensive insurance coverage. We can provide copies of our licenses and insurance certificates upon request.

    What is your payment schedule?

    We typically work with a payment schedule that includes an initial deposit, progress payments tied to project milestones, and a final payment upon completion. Specific terms are outlined in your contract.

    Do you work with architects and designers?

    Yes, we have in-house architects and designers, but we also collaborate with external professionals. We can work with your existing architect or designer, or recommend trusted partners.

    What areas do you serve?

    We primarily serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding counties. For projects outside our standard service area, we may be able to accommodate depending on project size and scope.

    Do you offer warranties on your work?

    Yes, we provide warranties on our workmanship. The specific warranty terms vary by project type and are detailed in your contract. We also honor manufacturer warranties on materials and fixtures.

    Can you work within my budget?

    We work with a wide range of budgets and can help you prioritize features to get the most value. During consultation, we'll discuss your budget and suggest options that align with your financial goals.
