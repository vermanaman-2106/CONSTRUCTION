About Us

Our Story

Engineers & Builders is a Greater Noida–based construction company founded by experienced engineers with 10+ years of expertise in industrial, civil, and interior works. Though a new venture, our team has already delivered many projects across India, especially for leading Korean multinational clients.

We bring together precision, innovation, and global standards, blending Korean quality benchmarks with Indian execution strength. With a strong professional team and proven capability, we aim to deliver turnkey industrial solutions that ensure speed, quality, and client satisfaction every time.

Our Vision

To be India’s most trusted and preferred construction partner for global industrial projects, delivering world-class infrastructure with precision, speed, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To build long-term partnerships with clients through integrity, accountability, and client-first execution. To build and maintain a highly skilled, safety-conscious workforce capable of rapid deployment across India.

Our Values

Building Beyond Standards which evolve from engineers into trusted builders, delivering turnkey industrial, civil, and interior projects with quality, precision, and global standards.
